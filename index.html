Julio Mendoza

AI Researcher | Data Scientist | Computer Vision Specialist

I am an Artificial Intelligence (AI) researcher and Data Scientist. I recently worked as AI researcher at the Samsung R&D Institute Brazil where he developed solutions for health and wellness for smartphone and wearable devices

My expertise is backed by deep academic research. I earned a Ph.D. in Computer Science from the University of Campinas, where I researched methods for understanding video scenes without direct supervision. I also got a Master's, also at the University of Campinas, where I applied Convolutional Neural Networks to medical imaging for Lung Nodule Detection. I holds a BE in Systems Engineering from the National University of San Agustin (UNSA).

Prior to my specialization in AI, I gained practical software engineering experience through roles as a software engineer at zAgile and a software developer at Scylla Informatica. Passionate about AI and Computer Vision community, I also co-organized the first LatinX in Computer Vision (LXCV) workshop at CVPR 2021.
